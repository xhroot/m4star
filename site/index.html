MCRN4star is a Call of Duty Mobile clan that focuses on Battle Royale

We're primarily a BR clan, but on occasion we enjoy playing together in various multiplayer modes.

Team Chat

We use Line chat app to communicate offline. Download the app to your phone, create an account, and click "Team Chat" above to join the MCRN4star clan channel.

Clan Guidelines

Turn on voice chat
This greatly assists team coordination and generally improves gameplay. Plus, it's more fun to chat while we slay.
Clan tag prefix: M4*
Inspire fear in your opponents! Identify with other clan members! Even bots lie down and accept death when they see us approach! Clan tag is optional but encouraged.
Treat each other kindly
Recognize and respect each other. Celebrate our diversity and unique talents we bring to the game.
